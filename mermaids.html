Appearance

Mermaids are usually portrayed as creatures with the head and torso of a woman, and the tail of a fish. They are depicted as beautiful creatures

Portrayal in Literature

The Little Mermaid

The Original Version

The book “The Little Mermaid” by Hans Christian Anderson was published in 1837.

Disney’s Adaptation

In 1989, Disney published an animated movie named “The Little Mermaid” that was based off of Hans Christian Anderson’s novel. The movie follows Ariel, a sixteen year old mermaid who longs to be human and immerse herself in the human world.
